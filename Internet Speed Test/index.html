Understanding Your Speed Test Results

Download Speed

This measures how fast you can pull data from the internet to your device. Higher download speeds mean faster web browsing, streaming, and file downloads.

Upload Speed

This measures how fast you can send data from your device to the internet. Important for video calls, uploading files, and live streaming.

Ping/Latency

This measures the delay between your device and the internet. Lower ping means more responsive online gaming and video calls.

Tips for Better Speed

For accurate results, close other applications, connect via ethernet if possible, and test multiple times throughout the day.
